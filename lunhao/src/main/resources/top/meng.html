Thanks mom for bringing me to this wonderful world

Hi, my name is Kwok Yin Mak. I used to go by Alex because it is easier than Kwok for most people, now I only use it when I order in public.

I was born in Hong Kong, raised in Florida, and currently spending my life in New York. I went to school for graphic design, but I learned most of the things from the internet and people around me.

Right now I am working at Ueno as a designer, which means I get to work with great people on cool projects. Use referral code kwok2019 when you apply so I can get a referral bonus.

I hate working alone, so please contact me if you are looking for collaborations, or it's cool to just say hi too.

hi@kwokyinmak.com / @kwokidile

大家好，我是麥國然。我出生在香港，十二歲時移民到美國佛羅里達州，現在居住在紐約。我在大學時是讀平面設計的，但多數我學的東西是從互聯網和我周圍的人。

我目前在Ueno擔任設計師。我非常幸運地能夠與很棒的人一起工作。如果您有興趣加入我們，求職時請使用推薦代碼kwok2019，哪我便能夠獲得推薦獎金。

我討厭獨自工作，如果你正在尋找合作機會，歡迎隨時與我聯繫交流，或者打個招呼。

電郵 / @kwokidile

Currently reading White Tears/Brown Scars by Ruby Hamad
